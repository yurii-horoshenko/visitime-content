## Tweet 3/7

So I opened every booking app on the App Store.

Filter by price. ✅
Filter by rating. ✅
Filter by distance. ✅
Filter by language. ❌
Filter by gender. ❌

The two filters that actually matter for service work — missing everywhere.

## Tweet 4/7

Millions of people live outside their home country. Millions prefer a master of a specific gender for personal services.

This is not a niche. This is most of a city like Warsaw, Berlin, London, Toronto.

Existing booking apps pretend these filters don't need to exist.

---

## Tweet 5/7

So I'm building VisiTime.

A booking app for any service — haircut, nails, therapy, tutor, massage — where you filter by language, gender, location, price. Then book, pay, show up.

Not beauty-only. Any service where a human touches another human.
