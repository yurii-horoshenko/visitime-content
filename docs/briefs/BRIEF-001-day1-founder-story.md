## Story arc

**1. Hook — the absurd moment**
I moved from Ukraine to Poland. I needed a haircut. Finding a barber who speaks my language took me three weeks of Instagram DMs, WhatsApp messages, and phone calls in a language I barely speak.

**2. The realization**
I'm not the only one. Millions of people live outside their home country. When you're in a chair with scissors near your head, "almost understand each other" is not good enough.

**3. Wider truth**
Every booking app today filters by price, rating, distance. None filter by **language** or **gender**. Those are the filters that actually matter for service work — hair, nails, therapy, tutoring.

**4. The product**
VisiTime — a booking app for any service, with filters that real humans actually use: language, gender, location, price. iOS, Android, one KMM codebase, .NET backend. Building it solo.

**5. The public part**
I'm building in public. Every decision, every screw-up. I have 13 AI agents on my team (more on that tomorrow).

**6. CTA**
